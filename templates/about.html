About Us

We are students of Orchid International College, Anuj, Milan, and Samir, working on building a sales prediction system for Big Mart using machine learning algorithms. Our project aims to predict future sales trends and provide valuable insights to optimize business strategies.

Utilizing the power of machine learning, we analyze historical sales data and various influencing factors to generate accurate predictions for future sales performance. With our combined expertise and dedication, we strive to deliver a reliable and innovative solution that meets the needs of our clients.

At Orchid International College, we are passionate about leveraging technology to drive positive change and solve real-world problems. Through our project, we aim to demonstrate our skills, creativity, and commitment to excellence in the field of data science and predictive analytics.
